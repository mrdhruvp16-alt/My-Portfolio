001ABOUT

THE WRITER.

I write the way most people talk to themselves at 2am. Half formed, a little too honest, looking for the line that makes the feeling sit still long enough to look at.

Just paper, a phone notes app, and whatever wouldn't leave me alone that day. These poems are filed exactly as they were found.
